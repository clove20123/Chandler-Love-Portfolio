Who I am

Developer based in Munich Germany

Hi, I’m Chandler Love, a junior full-stack web developer based in Munich Germany. I have experience in sales, where my role was sales representative. I specialized in acquiring customers, helping resolve customer complaints, and provided a link between customers and management. In this role I learned how to build strong relationships, deal with problems in a fast and efficient mannor, and most importantly listen. After spending 7 years in the field, I decided to pursue my passion which was web development. Within a year I had completed a full-stack web development course where I created interesting projects that ranged from APIs to mobile chat applications. Since completing the course I have continued to work on new projects to broaden my knowledge of the web dev world.

I am a laid-back guy from North Carolina, USA that enjoys playing golf, gaming, and cooking. My wife and I have two dogs that shed and wreak havoc, but we still love them. I am a big family guy so always staying in touch with my family even though they are in the USA. If you want to know anything else about me reach out, I’m always happy to chat.
